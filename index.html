Information We Collect

Fast Chat collects and receives information when we operate our Services, including when you download and install, access, or use our Services. This may include, but not limited to: Account information. You will provide your email address to create a Fast Chat account. You may also add information to your account, such as profile or username and profile picture. Messages. We will not store or retain your messages in the course of providing our services to you. Once your messages have been delivered and received, they will be deleted from our servers. Your messages will be stored on your local mobile device. Connections. To personalize and enhance your messaging experience, you will be able to import your contacts, create or join groups, or get added to groups by other users of the app. Customer Support. We may contact you through your given email or you may contact us through an email when regarding issues or app performance.

How We Use Information

We use all the information we collect and receive to provide, maintain, protect and improve our Services. When contacting us, we may use your email to communicate with you if you face any issues and inform you about upcoming changes to the app. We will ask for consent if we intend to use your information for anything not specified within this Privacy Policy.

Managing Your Information

If you would like to access, manage, change, limit, or delete your information, we allow you to do so through the app tools and functions: Service Settings. Through the Service Settings, you are able to manage, change, or limit how much information is shared or available to other users. You can manage, your contacts, chat groups, or block other users. Changing your profile information. You can change your username or profile picture at any time within the Profile Settings. You may also change your email address if needed, but we may ask you to verify your identity through a confirmation email. Deleting your account. You may delete your Fast Chat account at any time.

Updates To Our Privacy Policy

We may amend or update this Privacy Policy. We will post privacy policy changes within the app and will notify you with an in-app notification. It will prompt you for your approval and that you understand these changes. Each privacy policy will be dated, as well.
